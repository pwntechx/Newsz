RECIPE 01
Egg & Rice Bowl
5 minutes · 1 bowl · microwave
ACTIVE TIME
2 min
TOTAL TIME
5 min
COST
~$1.20

Ingredients

  • 1 cup instant rice (microwave pouch or bulk)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp soy sauce (or salt)
  • Optional: 1/4 cup frozen peas

Instructions

  1. Cook rice in microwave according to package (usually 90 sec).
  2. Crack egg into same bowl. Stir into hot rice.
  3. Add frozen peas if using.
  4. Microwave 60 sec. (Start 45 sec, check, add 15 sec if needed.)
  5. Add soy sauce. Stir. Eat.
✦ Upgrade: Fry egg in pan (3 min) and place on top. Different texture, same effort trade-off.

RECIPE 02
Loaded Microwave Potato
8 minutes · 1 bowl · microwave
ACTIVE TIME
3 min
TOTAL TIME
8 min
COST
~$1.00

Ingredients

  • 1 medium potato (russet or sweet)
  • 1/4 cup cottage cheese or Greek yogurt
  • Salt, black pepper, garlic powder
  • Optional: 1 Tbsp shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Wash potato. Pierce with fork 3-4 times (or wrap in damp paper towel, skip piercing).
  2. Microwave 5-9 min depending on size (fist: 5 min, medium: 7 min, large: 9 min). Squeeze to test — should be soft.
  3. Remove. Split open with fork (or leave whole).
  4. Top with cottage cheese/yogurt, salt, pepper, garlic powder.
  5. Add cheese if using. Eat from potato skin as bowl.
✦ Upgrade: Add canned beans, frozen broccoli, or fried egg. For no-fridge: use canned beans instead of dairy.

RECIPE 03
Sardine Toast
5 minutes · no cooking · no microwave
ACTIVE TIME
3 min
TOTAL TIME
5 min
COST
~$1.80

Ingredients

  • 1 slice bread (toasted or not)
  • 1/2 can sardines in oil or water (2-3 fish)
  • 1/4 cup shredded cabbage or handful spinach
  • 1 tsp soy sauce or squeeze of lemon
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Toast bread if desired (fine untoasted or microwave 15 sec).
  2. Open sardines. Drain liquid. (Look for pull-tab cans.)
  3. Place cabbage or spinach on bread.
  4. Lay sardines on top.
  5. Add soy sauce/lemon, pepper. Eat open-faced.
✦ Upgrade: Mash sardines with yogurt or mayo, spread like tuna salad. Add hot sauce.

RECIPE 04
Peanut Butter Noodles
10 minutes · 1 pot
ACTIVE TIME
3 min
TOTAL TIME
10 min
COST
~$1.10

Ingredients

  • 1 packet ramen noodles (discard seasoning) or 2 oz pasta
  • 1 Tbsp peanut butter (natural or regular)
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • Optional: 1/4 cup frozen peas or edamame

Instructions

  1. Boil water in pot (or microwave water in bowl, add noodles, microwave 4-5 min).
  2. Cook noodles according to package.
  3. In bowl, mix peanut butter, soy sauce, garlic powder.
  4. Add 2 Tbsp hot noodle water to sauce. Stir until smooth.
  5. Drain noodles (use fork to lift out if no colander). Add to sauce. Toss.
  6. Add peas if using. Eat.
✦ Upgrade: Add soft boiled egg, canned sardines, or crushed peanuts on top.

RECIPE 05
Lentil & Rice Bowl
20 minutes · 1 pot · stove
ACTIVE TIME
5 min
TOTAL TIME
20 min
COST
~$0.80

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup red lentils
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup instant rice (or leftover rice)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Optional: 1/4 cup frozen spinach

Instructions

  1. Rinse lentils in pot (add water, swirl, pour out carefully — repeat 2-3 times).
  2. Add water, turmeric, salt. Bring to boil.
  3. Reduce to low. Simmer 10 minutes (bubbles should be gentle).
  4. Add rice, frozen spinach, garlic powder. Simmer 5 more minutes.
  5. Stir. Eat from pot (less dishes) or transfer to bowl.
✦ Upgrade: Add fried egg on top, spoon of yogurt, or squeeze of lemon.
