§ 00

DESIGN DOCTRINE

Perplexity gave you a correct starter kit. Rice, lentils, peanut butter, honey — those are real. But that stack was designed for one scenario: stationary, cooking available, single person. It fails the hybrid constraint on day one.

The architecture here is built on four non-negotiable axioms:

AXIOM SET
1. WATER IS THE REAL CONSTRAINT — food without water access kills faster than no food.
2. PHASE SEPARATION — 72hr mobile kit and 30-day shelter core are different systems, not the same list scaled up.
3. NO SINGLE POINT OF FAILURE — if one food category is unavailable, spoiled, or lost, the system continues.
4. NUTRITION IS NOT JUST CALORIES — micronutrient depletion at week 2 degrades decision-making and immune function. That is a mission-critical failure.

The stack is organized in two phases. Phase 1 is your mobile 72-hour kit — highest caloric density per gram, zero cooking, fits in a 30L pack per person. Phase 2 is your shelter core — volume-optimized, longer prep options, nutritional completeness for the full 30 days.

PHASE 1 — 72HR MOBILE KIT

Weight is the enemy. Zero cooking. Max 15 lbs total per person including water. Every item must be eat-as-is or cold-soak only. Shelf stable minimum 2 years.

Pemmican / Meat Bars
3500 CAL/LB · NO COOK · 5YR SHELF

The highest caloric density real food that exists. Fat + protein, no carbohydrate crash. Used by Arctic explorers and military for exactly this scenario. Not jerky — pemmican. The difference is the rendered fat content that doubles the caloric density.

Single-serve squeeze packets — no jar to carry, no utensil needed. Almond + peanut mix for amino acid breadth. Fat and protein that sustains rather than spikes. These are the fat anchor of the mobile phase.

1600 CAL/LB · 30YR SHELF · DENSE

PHASE 2 — SHELTER CORE

CONSTRAINT
Weight no longer the binding constraint. Volume and nutritional completeness now dominate. Minimal cooking acceptable — single burner or rocket stove. Target: 30-day full nutritional sufficiency for 2–4 crew.

1600 CAL/LB · 30YR SEALED · CALORIC BASE

The caloric backbone. Sealed in Mylar with oxygen absorbers, white rice stores 25–30 years. The most calories per dollar of any food on Earth. Requires cooking — but minimal fuel. One cup rice, two cups water, 20 minutes. If no heat available, cold-soak for 4 hours.

STORE: 40 lbs for 4 people / 30 days
CALS: ~64,000 total
COLD SOAK: Viable if absolutely no heat

COMPLETE PROTEIN WITH RICE · FAST COOK

Red lentils specifically — they cook in 15 minutes versus 45 for green or brown. When combined with rice you get all nine essential amino acids. This is the protein architecture of the shelter phase. Cold soak 8 hours makes them edible without heat as a backup.

STORE: 20 lbs for 4 people / 30 days
CALS: ~32,000 total
PROTEIN: ~25g per cooked cup
SHELTER-03 · CRITICAL
Peanut Butter (Commercial)
2600 CAL/LB · NO COOK · FAT ANCHOR

The no-cook fat and protein anchor of the shelter phase. Stabilized commercial peanut butter — not natural — stores 2 years unopened. A tablespoon is 90 calories. Eat it straight, mix into rice, spread on hardtack. The most calorie-dense thing in the shelter core that requires zero preparation.

Coconut oil over olive oil for this scenario — it's solid at room temperature which prevents spill and spoilage, contains MCTs for cognitive function, and has a longer shelf life once opened. Add to everything. One tablespoon per meal adds 120 calories and fat-soluble vitamin absorption.

One multivitamin per person per day is a near-zero weight, near-zero cost insurance policy on decision-making capacity.

Sardines packed in olive oil are the highest omega-3 density food available in a shelf-stable format. Omega-3s are anti-inflammatory — critical when the stress response is chronically elevated. Complete protein, calcium from the bones, vitamin D. Eat from the can. No preparation required.

STORE: 48 cans for 4 people / 30 days

A tablespoon a day improves mineral absorption from the rice and lentils — the phytic acid in grains blocks iron and zinc absorption, and ACV partially neutralizes it. Also a preservation agent and a flavor addition that costs nothing in weight relative to its function.

Turmeric, cumin, garlic powder, chili flakes, black pepper. This is not comfort food — it's anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial function plus the palatability that sustains voluntary eating across 30 days. People stop eating when food is too monotonous. That is a real caloric collapse risk.

§ 03

WATER — THE ACTUAL CONSTRAINT

WATER IS THE MISSION

A human being dies of dehydration in 3 days. Of starvation in 3 weeks. The food stack is irrelevant if the water architecture fails. This section is not optional.

For a 30-day shelter scenario with 4 crew: drinking alone requires 120 gallons. Cooking and sanitation doubles that. You are not storing 240 gallons. You are storing the means to source and purify water continuously.

STORED SUPPLY (72HR BUFFER): 12 gallons minimum (3L/person/day × 4 people × 3 days)
FILTRATION: Sawyer Squeeze or Berkey — removes 99.99% bacteria/protozoa. Mandatory.
CHEMICAL BACKUP: Aquatabs (sodium dichloroisocyanurate) — 100 tablets per person
BOILING BACKUP: Any heat source + pot = safe water. Third layer of redundancy.
COLLECTION: 4× collapsible 5-gallon containers for rain/source collection
UV STERILIZATION: SteriPen or similar — batteries required, tertiary only

RULE: 3 independent water purification methods. If one fails, you have two left. E=1.
§ 04

NUTRITIONAL MEDICINE

These are not supplements in the wellness sense. They are gap-fillers for documented deficiencies that emerge in a restricted diet under high stress over 30 days.

MED-01 · CRITICAL
Vitamin C (1000mg tabs)
SCURVY PREVENTION · IMMUNE · COLLAGEN

Scurvy onset begins at 4–6 weeks of deficiency. The food stack has minimal C. 1000mg/day per person. Non-negotiable. Cognitive decline and wound healing failure are the early symptoms.

STORE: 120 tabs · WEIGHT: Near zero
ONSET WITHOUT: 4–6 weeks
MED-02 · CRITICAL
Vitamin D3 + K2
IMMUNE · CALCIUM REGULATION · MOOD

Shelter-in-place means no sunlight. D3 deficiency in 2–3 weeks affects immune function, mood regulation, and calcium metabolism. K2 ensures D3-driven calcium goes to bones, not arteries. The combination matters.

STORE: 120 softgels · WEIGHT: Near zero
CRITICAL IN: No-sunlight scenarios
MED-03 · HIGH
Magnesium Glycinate
STRESS RESPONSE · SLEEP · MUSCLE

Stress depletes magnesium faster than any other mineral. Low magnesium impairs sleep, elevates cortisol, causes muscle cramping. 400mg/night per person. Glycinate form — highest absorption, no laxative effect.

STORE: 120 caps · WEIGHT: Near zero
TAKE: At night, improves sleep quality under stress
MED-04 · HIGH
Zinc + Copper
IMMUNE · WOUND HEALING · COGNITION

Phytic acid in rice and lentils blocks zinc absorption significantly. 15mg zinc + 1mg copper daily per person. Copper prevents zinc-induced copper deficiency. The ratio matters. Immune function and wound healing both require zinc at crisis-relevant timescales.

STORE: 120 tabs · WEIGHT: Near zero
RATIO: 15mg Zn to 1mg Cu — always paired
