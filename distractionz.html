THE NODES OF CAPTURE

The Colosseum, the feed, the scroll, the narrative — they are not separate. They are nodes in a single extraction machine. Your attention is the fuel. Your engagement is the product. Your immobility is the goal.

THE Colosseum

Bread and circuses. The state funds the arena; the arena controls the crowd. Spectacle as opiate — full bellies, distracted eyes. The crowd is unified, emotional, easily directed. Violence as entertainment normalizes suffering. You watch. You cheer. You do not notice the walls.

Who speaks? Whose suffering is visible? Whose is invisible? The victor writes history. The algorithm amplifies the profitable. The marginalized are silenced not by force but by irrelevance. The meta-narrative paralyzes: "both sides have valid points." "It is complicated." Action becomes impossible.
